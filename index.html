Kronosnet

High Availability Network Abstraction Layer

PROVIDE A RELIABLE, RESILIENT, SCALABLE, STABLE, HIGHLY CONFIGURABLE, SECURE AND SIMPLE NETWORK ABSTRACTION LAYER

Kronosnet (knet) is a network abstraction layer designed for High Availability use cases, where redundancy, security, fault tolerance and fast fail-over are the core requirements.

Open Source Forever

Kronosnet is and will always remain 100% open source. We believe in transparency, community collaboration, and the freedom to use, modify, and distribute our software. Licensed under GPL-2.0+ for applications and LGPL-2.1+ for libraries, kronosnet is built by the community, for the community.
